Products & Services

FUEL DELIVERY

Trinity Energy provides fuel products to stationary installations, infrastructure, and generator sites. Based on customer needs, we offer bespoke logistics and supply chain solutions to ensure the delivery of fuel products to remote or even risk prone locations. Our fuel storage solutions allow us to deliver an uninterrupted supply of bulk fuel to our customer sites.

ON-SITE FUELING SOLUTIONS:

Trinity Energy can arrange custom on-site storage solutions for clients looking to leverage our existing capabilities without making a substantial capital investment themselves.

BULK FUEL SUPPLY:

Trinity Energy supplies fuel in bulk to large consumers, downstream oil marketing companies, and retail filling stations. By addressing our customers fuel procurement and logistics needs, we enable our customers to focus on their core business. Customers with self-managed distribution and supply chains can purchase fuel in large quantities directly from our depot at Nesitu. Customers using this service enjoy the ability to buy quality tested fuel products in bulk without needing to arrange for cross-border transportation.

RETAIL:

Trinity Energy with an ever growing retail network across East and Central Africa is able to serve retail consumers with quality fuel and lubricants.

Organizational customers can arrange fuel purchasing agreements with Trinity Energy to offer drivers and fleet vehicles flexible fueling options wherever we are present.

Our robust supply chain allows our stations to maintain fuel availability even in times of fuel supply challenges.
